Getting Started

Welcome aboard! 👋

Here's what happens next to get your website protected.

✓
Account created
Your ProCyberWall account is ready to go.
2
Share your domain with us
WhatsApp us your website URL (e.g. shopkaro.in). We'll add it to our system and configure firewall rules for your site.
3
Update your nameservers
We'll send you two nameserver values. Log in to wherever you bought your domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, BigRock, etc.) and replace the existing nameservers with the ones we send. Takes 2 minutes — we'll walk you through it if needed.
4
Protection goes live
DNS changes take 10 minutes to 24 hours to propagate. Once done, your firewall is active and your dashboard will show live threat data. We'll WhatsApp you the moment it's live.
